Rules:
   1. Each player is dealt five cards.
   2. Players bet; each player may fold, see the current bet, or raise.
   3. In turn, each player can choose to discard up to three cards.
         -> They are dealt new cards from the deck to replace their old cards.
   4. Players bet again.
   5. If any players do not fold, then players reveal their hands; strongest hand wins the pot.
